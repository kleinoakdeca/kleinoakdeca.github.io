Mission Statement

DECA prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs for careers in marketing, finance, hospitality, and managment in highschools and colleges around the globe. With over two hundred thousand members, DECA continues to inspire students who are driven and dedicated.

Local Chapter

Klein Oak DECA members compete and succesfully place in district and state levels, as well as advancing to international competition. The chapter's success is reflected in its steady growth of membership for the past handful of years. The chapter promotes the development of leadership qualities as well dedication and hardwork.
